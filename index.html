About me

Robotics Engineer/Front End Developer

I am a 3rd Year Engineering Science student specializing in Robotics at the University of Toronto. My journey has been marked by a blend of technical and innovative research. I have developed an Implantable piezoelectric energy harvester and conducted advanced DFT analysis to enhance lithium-ion battery performance. This year, my team designed a pneumatic waste management system for Bangladesh, demonstrating my ability to address global challenges with creative solutions. Beyond my technical achievements, I am dedicated to mentoring younger engineering students, actively contributing to a design team, and am currently pursuing a Front-end Developer certification from Meta. My vision is to harness my expertise in robotics and engineering to create sustainable technologies that significantly impact the world.
